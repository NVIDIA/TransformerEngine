Testing and Engineering Quality
===============================

The practices used to establish correctness, compatibility, reliability, and
performance before a change is accepted.

Planned coverage
----------------

* Test organization, selection, and canonical commands.
* Writing tests and choosing appropriate test levels.
* Numerical references, tolerances, stochastic behavior, and determinism.
* Continuous-integration topology and local reproduction.
* Debugging tools and troubleshooting workflows.
* Profiling, benchmarking, and performance-regression evaluation.
* API, ABI, checkpoint, hardware, and software compatibility policies.
